A six-part series following the migration of a Symfony platform from Docker Compose to Kubernetes. Each article starts from a concrete problem — a rate limiter that gave different answers depending on the pod, logs that vanished before a crash, credentials compiled into Docker layers — and follows it to its resolution through the twelve-factor methodology.
